HOAKSIBER

is a learning game for teens on how to prevent the hoax. In the era of the technology, informations are easily get, and share to the social media platforms is the easiest way to spread informations. But not all the informations are verified, some of those are marked as hoax, and it have specific characteristics.

This is a game which shows a person in a very dark labyrinth, and in each crossing way, he gets a newinformation. The information will be his new step for another way. The labyrinth represents as how internet is wide, but we cannot know what's inside it, and the crossing way represents how much our way of responding the informations, should we share it or not. If we choose the wrong step, we'll get drowned into hoaxes.

Competition Purpose

Third Place in provincial Edusiber Coding Competition
